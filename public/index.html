How it works

  1. Someone asks for help.
  2. Local student volunteers get matched.
  3. Work is completed and hours are certified.

What happens next

  • An admin or trusted review flow checks the request.
  • Nearby students who fit the task get notified.
  • The requester confirms completion and volunteer hours.
First version idea: keep this simple, safe, and easy to approve.

Profiles

Create an account based on whether you want to help or need help.

Student volunteer profile

  • Name, school, graduation year, and guardian contact
  • Availability, transportation radius, and interests
  • Volunteer hour history and verified completion log
Example: Jordan, Junior, available Tue/Thu/Sat, interested in elderly support and tech help.

Help request profile

  • Name, address area, and whether a caregiver is present
  • Accessibility details and safe task guidelines
  • History of past requests and volunteer confirmations
Example: Ms. Rivera, caregiver-assisted, requests light moving, grocery unloading, and seasonal cleanup.
