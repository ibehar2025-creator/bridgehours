Why this matters

Students often need meaningful service hours. Communities already have real needs. This site turns both sides into one organized system.

Core concept

Simple enough to launch, strong enough to grow.

01

Someone posts a need

A resident, caregiver, nonprofit, or family member submits a task like yard cleanup, carrying groceries, organizing a room, or moving light boxes.

02

Students get matched

Nearby volunteers browse the board or receive matches based on availability, age limits, task type, and transportation range.

03

Hours are certified

After the task is completed, the request owner confirms attendance and the platform generates verified volunteer-hour records.

Trust and safety

The site should make parents, schools, and neighbors feel comfortable.

Verified users

Student profiles, guardians, schools, and requesters can all be verified in stages.

Task guidelines

Clear rules can block unsafe work, heavy labor, and tasks needing professional licenses.

Hour approval

Every completed task gets reviewed before hours are added to a student record.

School-ready proof

Verified logs can eventually export into a signed PDF or counselor-friendly format.

What to build next

This starter site is a good first framework for pitching and testing the idea.
